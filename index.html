What is the
StoryLab Academy?

Journalism is evolving so quickly that newsrooms are struggling to keep up.

The StoryLab Academy seeks to help, by bringing face-to-face training into partner newsrooms across Africa, and by hosting public workshops at monthly Hacks/Hackers meetups.

The Academy will also offer online courses and webinars, designed to teach just one tool or technique at a time, so participants can upgrade their skills at their own pace.
